# Channel Selection Framework

## Channel Scoring (each 1-10)

| Dimension | 1 | 5 | 10 |
|-----------|---|---|-----|
| **Reach** | <100 potential users | 1K-10K | 100K+ |
| **Cost** | $100+ per acquisition | $10-50 | <$1 |
| **Founder-fit** | Requires skills you don't have | Learnable | Natural strength |
| **Speed** | 6+ months to signal | 1-3 months | <2 weeks |

**Total = sum of 4 dimensions.** Max 40. Rank channels by total.

## Channel Categories

### Content (blogs, tutorials, videos)
- **Best for:** Developer tools, knowledge products, anything where expertise = trust
- **Speed:** Slow (3-6 months to compound)
- **Cost:** Time only
- **Signal:** traffic → signups → activation. If no activation, content is reaching wrong people.

### Community (forums, Discord, Slack, meetups)
- **Best for:** Niche products, tools with workflows
- **Speed:** Medium (1-3 months)
- **Cost:** Time + emotional energy
- **Signal:** questions about your category = demand signal. Answer with your product.

### Marketplace / directory
- **Best for:** Plugins, integrations, tools with platform dependencies
- **Speed:** Fast (days to weeks for listing, months for traction)
- **Cost:** Listing effort
- **Signal:** installs, ratings, reviews

### Direct outreach (cold email, DM, LinkedIn)
- **Best for:** B2B, high-value products, consulting-to-product transition
- **Speed:** Fastest (days)
- **Cost:** Time per lead
- **Signal:** reply rate >5% = message works. <2% = wrong audience or wrong message.

### Social (Twitter/X, HN, Reddit, Product Hunt)
- **Best for:** Products with visual or demo-able value
- **Speed:** Unpredictable (viral or nothing)
- **Cost:** Time + consistency
- **Signal:** engagement → clicks → signups. Engagement without clicks = entertainment, not distribution.

### SEO
- **Best for:** Products solving searchable problems ("how to X")
- **Speed:** Very slow (6-12 months)
- **Cost:** Content creation
- **Signal:** ranking → traffic → signups. Only works if people search for your problem.

### Partnerships
- **Best for:** Products complementary to established tools
- **Speed:** Slow (relationship building)
- **Cost:** Integration effort
- **Signal:** referral traffic, co-marketing reach

## Solo Founder Channel Prioritization

1. **Where are your users already?** Go there. Don't build a new audience.
2. **One channel at a time.** Trying 3 channels = doing none well.
3. **Fastest validation first.** Direct outreach > content marketing for first 10 users.
4. **Match your strengths.** Can you write? Content. Can you network? Community. Can you demo? Social.
5. **Kill after 30 days.** If a channel shows zero signal in 30 days, it's wrong for you right now.
