# Strategy Frameworks

## Stage-Appropriate Work
| Stage | Focus | Anti-pattern |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| mvp/one | One feature, full depth. Get one person to value. | Spreading across 3+ features |
| early/some | Retention. Polish existing. | Building new before existing works |
| growth/many | Distribution, onboarding. | Features over distribution |
| mature | Expansion, enterprise. | Rebuilding core |

## Bet Scoring Dimensions (7)
1. Problem severity (1-10): How much does this hurt?
2. Solution quality (1-10): Does this actually solve it?
3. Market size (1-10): How many people have this problem?
4. Timing (1-10): Why now?
5. Founder fit (1-10): Why you?
6. Moat potential (1-10): What prevents copying?
7. Distribution (1-10): How do users find this?

Anti-sycophancy gate: minimum 2 dimensions must score below 5. If everything scores 7+, you're lying to yourself.

## Honest Mode Questions
- What are you avoiding?
- What would a smart skeptic say?
- If you had to bet your own money, how much?
- What would you build if you started over today?
