## Confidence Levels
- **WEAK** — Single source, unverified. May be noise.
- **STRONG** — 2+ independent sources in same session, or confirmed across 2 sessions.
- **CONFIRMED** — 3+ sessions of consistent evidence. Stop researching, start acting.
- **DISPROVEN** — Contradicting evidence found. Mark and skip in future sessions.
