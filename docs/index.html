What it does

rhino-os gives Claude Code a brain. Instead of a blank-slate assistant, you get an opinionated system that tracks your portfolio, learns your taste, scores your product, and autonomously decides what to build next.

strategy

Portfolio intelligence

Buy/Sell/Hold verdicts on your projects. Escape velocity checks. Sprint planning that starts from "what's the weakest dimension?" not "what do I feel like building?"

build

The one loop

Read state, decide scope, execute, measure, keep or discard. Five modes auto-detected: gate, plan, build, experiment, doctor. No process — just momentum.

scoring

Two-tier measurement

Training loss (grep-based, every commit, 5 dimensions) + eval loss (Playwright screenshots, Claude vision, 8 taste dimensions). Track trends, not averages.

knowledge

Compounding intelligence

Taste signals, market positions, design preferences, session artifacts. Agents read shared state — no human relay. Knowledge compounds across sessions.

autonomy

AI-first decisions

Agents ideate, research, and decide before escalating. Human approves irreversible actions. The system runs overnight and posts findings for morning review.

taste

Anti-convergence

IA/VA convergence detection fights "all AI apps look the same." Anti-slop rules loaded on every UI file. Taste is measured by looking, not grepping.

Design principles

Momentum over process

One builder handles all modes. Skip what you don't need. No ceremonies, no standups, no sprint retrospectives.

Minimal manual input

Agents read shared state automatically. No human relay between agents. The filesystem IS the message bus.

Knowledge compounds

Taste signals, market positions, design preferences persist and strengthen. By week four, agents know your style.

Measure by looking

Don't grep for quality. Screenshot the product and judge what you see. Training loss is cheap; eval loss is real.

Subtractive design

Started with 13 agents, ended with 2 programs. The system deletes itself into shape. Less surface area = less confusion.

Safety by default

Budget caps on agents. Human approves RED items. No auto-deploy. Irreversible actions require confirmation.
