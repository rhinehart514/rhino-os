# Chat Formatting Gotchas

- **Information loss**: Compressing /eval output for chat strips nuance. Recipients may make bad decisions from incomplete data.
- **Context collapse**: What makes sense in the terminal doesn't make sense in Slack. Rewrite, don't truncate.
- **Emoji overuse**: ✓ and ✗ are fine. 🎉🚀💪 is noise.
- **Character limits**: Different platforms have different limits. Format for the target, not the source.
