# Product Thinking Gotchas

- **Pressure-testing gently**: The whole point of /product is to be harsh. If the conclusion is always "yes, build it," the test isn't working.
- **Assumption invisibility**: The most dangerous assumptions are the ones you don't know you're making.
- **Building for yourself**: "I would use this" ≠ anyone else would. Name the specific person who isn't you.
- **Market research as validation**: Finding that a market exists doesn't validate YOUR product in it.
- **Coherence as completeness**: A coherent narrative isn't evidence of a good product. Bad products can have great stories.
