# Roadmap Gotchas

- **Thesis vs implementation confusion**: State what you PROVED, not what you BUILT. "Implemented assertions" is not the thesis.
- **Evidence item staleness**: Items marked "partial" months ago are forgotten. Revisit or declare dead.
- **Narrative fiction**: Marketing copy overstates what was proven. Changelog should reflect eval scores, not aspirations.
- **Version inflation**: Bumping major for minor work. Major = new thesis. Minor = significant improvement. Patch = fix.
- **Roadmap as wishlist**: Future versions with no connection to current evidence are fiction.
- **Positioning assumptions**: Assumes you know your market. Often you don't. Use /strategy market first.
