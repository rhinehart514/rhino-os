# Skill Management Gotchas

- **Creating without evidence**: The 3-question gate exists because most skill ideas don't survive contact with reality. If the founder can't point to 3+ sessions where the pattern appeared, too early.
- **Overlap blindness**: Two skills that do 60% the same thing fragment intelligence. Always check overlap before creating.
- **Structure without measurement**: A 400-line SKILL.md with 0 assertions is theater. Structure doesn't prove the skill works.
- **Thick skill vanity**: Not every skill needs to be thick. Low-weight, narrow-scope skills should stay thin. High-weight thin skills are the real problem.
- **Unmeasured survival**: Skills without assertions survive indefinitely. Flag at 30 days, kill or measure at 60.
- **Folder over-engineering**: Adding scripts/references/templates to a skill that runs once a month is waste. Match investment to usage frequency.
- **Route keyword overlap**: "analyze" and "evaluate" and "check" all mean similar things. Route overlap detection needs semantic matching, not just keyword matching.
